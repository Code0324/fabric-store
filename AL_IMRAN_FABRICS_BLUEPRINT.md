## 1. Brand & Positioning

### 1.1 Brand Story

**AL Imran Fabrics** was founded with a vision to preserve Pakistan's rich textile heritage while bringing premium fabrics to the modern consumer. Our name honors the legacy of craftsmanship passed down through generations—"AL Imran" signifying construction and legacy building.

For over a decade, we've been sourcing the finest fabrics from local mills, international suppliers, and artisanal weavers. Today, we're digitizing this experience, making premium textiles accessible to designers, tailors, homemakers, and fashion enthusiasts across Pakistan.

### 1.2 Mission, Vision & Values

**Mission:**
"To democratize access to premium fabrics by connecting skilled artisans, designers, and customers with authentic, high-quality textiles at fair prices."

**Vision:**
"To become Pakistan's most trusted online destination for fabrics, where heritage meets modernity, and every customer finds their perfect textile."

**Core Values:**
- **Authenticity:** Genuine fabrics, transparent sourcing
- **Quality First:** Rigorous quality control, no compromises
- **Heritage Pride:** Celebrating Pakistani textile traditions
- **Customer-Centric:** Listen, serve, exceed expectations
- **Sustainability:** Ethical sourcing, responsible practices

### 1.3 Tagline & Value Proposition

**Primary Tagline:**
"Premium Fabrics, Local Heritage, Modern Shopping"

**Alternative Taglines:**
- "Threads of Tradition, Touch of Modernity"
- "Your Perfect Fabric, Just a Click Away"
- "Quality Textiles for Every Vision"
- "From Mills to You - Direct, Premium, Authentic"

**Value Proposition:**
AL Imran Fabrics offers a curated selection of premium, authentic fabrics directly from manufacturers and artisans, eliminating middlemen and passing savings to customers. Every fabric is quality-checked, beautifully photographed, and accompanied by detailed specifications for informed purchasing. We serve everyone from fashion designers to home tailors with transparent pricing, flexible quantities, and expert guidance.

### 1.4 Brand Positioning

**Market Position:**
- Premium but not luxury (accessible to middle and upper-middle class)
- Local heritage + modern convenience
- Direct-to-consumer model ensures authenticity and fair pricing
- Expert-backed (textile knowledge, styling tips, tailoring guidance)

**Brand Personality:**
- **Sophisticated yet approachable** - professional but friendly
- **Heritage-conscious** - proud of textile traditions
- **Quality-obsessed** - meticulous about every detail
- **Helpful and knowledgeable** - guides customers through choices
- **Modern and convenient** - seamless digital experience

**Visual Identity Essence:**
- Elegant, minimalist design with fabric-inspired textures
- Warm, natural color palette reflecting fabrics and textiles
- High-quality photography showcasing fabric details
- Handwritten elements to convey craftsmanship

**Buttons:**
- **Primary:** Brown (#8B4513) background, white text, 12px padding, border-radius 4px
- **Secondary:** White background, brown border, brown text

- **Disabled:** Gray background, light text

**Input Fields:**
- Border: 1px solid #E0DDD8
- Padding: 10px 12px
- Font: Inter, 14px
- Focus: Brown border (2px), subtle shadow
- Label: 12px, bold, above field
- Error state: Red border, error message below

**Cards:**
- Background: White
- Border-radius: 8px
- Box-shadow: 0 2px 8px rgba(0,0,0,0.08)
- Hover: Slight shadow increase, scale 1.02

**Form Structure:**
- Field spacing: 16px
- Grouped related fields
- Progress indicators for multi-step forms
- Inline validation (✓/✗ icons)

**Navigation:**
- Active page: Underline + brown color
- Hover: Color change or light background
- Mobile: Hamburger menu with slide-out

**Modals:**
- Backdrop: Semi-transparent dark overlay
- Modal: White, rounded corners
- Close button (X) in top-right
- Centered on screen

### 7.4 Responsive Design

**Breakpoints:**
- Mobile: 320px - 767px
- Tablet: 768px - 1024px
- Desktop: 1025px+

**Responsive Adjustments:**
- Navigation: Hamburger (mobile) → Full menu (desktop)
- Grids: 2 columns (mobile) → 3 (tablet) → 4 (desktop)
- Images: Scaled to viewport, optimized sizes
- Text: Readable without zoom (min 16px on mobile)
- Touch targets: Min 48px (mobile)
- Modals: Full-screen (mobile) → Centered (desktop)

**Mobile Optimizations:**
- Simplified menus
- Touch-friendly buttons (44px minimum)
- Swipe gestures for galleries
- Collapsible filters
- Bottom sheet modals
- Streamlined checkout

### 7.5 Accessibility (WCAG 2.1 AA)

**Color Contrast:**
- Text: 4.5:1 ratio (normal text)
- Large text: 3:1 ratio
- Links: Distinguishable (not color alone)

**Typography:**
- Alt text for all images
- Heading hierarchy (H1 → H2 → H3)
- Semantic HTML (nav, main, section, article)
- Form labels properly associated

**Keyboard Navigation:**
- All elements accessible via Tab
- Focus visible (outline/highlight)
- Logical tab order (left-to-right, top-to-bottom)
- Close modals with Esc

**Screen Reader Support:**
- ARIA labels for icon-only buttons
- Form error announcements
- Live regions for dynamic content
- Skip navigation link

**Motion & Animation:**
- Respect prefers-reduced-motion
- Animations optional, not auto-play
- No rapid flashing (> 3/second)

### 10.5 Security Implementation

**Frontend Security:**
- Content Security Policy (CSP) headers
- CSRF token generation and validation
- Input sanitization and validation
- XSS prevention (React's auto-escaping)
- Secure cookies (httpOnly, secure, sameSite)

**Backend Security:**
- JWT tokens (15-min access, 7-day refresh)
- API rate limiting (100 requests/minute per IP)
- SQL injection prevention (parameterized queries via ORM)
- CORS properly configured
- Password hashing (bcrypt, 10+ salt rounds)
- Environment variables for secrets (.env, never committed)
- Request validation middleware

**Payment Security:**
- PCI DSS Level 1 compliance
- No card storage (tokenization via payment gateway)
- SSL/TLS for all communication
- 3D Secure (3DS) enabled for card payments
- Webhook signature verification for payment confirmations

**Data Protection:**
- Encryption at rest (database encryption)
- Encryption in transit (HTTPS everywhere)
- Regular security audits
- Vulnerability scanning (OWASP top 10)
- Database backups (daily, geo-replicated)
- User data deletion requests (GDPR compliance)
