## UX STRATEGY - STITCHED vs UNSTITCHED

| Element | Stitched | Unstitched |
|---------|----------|-----------|
| **Primary Image** | Model wearing | Fabric close-up/texture |
| **Size Selector** | S, M, L, XL | N/A (pre-cut pieces) |
| **Piece Info** | Not needed | Essential breakdown |
| **Design Focus** | Finished look | Customization potential |
| **Stitching Tabs** | Care Instructions | Stitching Tips + Design Ideas |
| **Related Items** | Similar stitched suits | Complementary fabrics, lining |
| **Target Buyer** | "Ready-to-wear" shoppers | "DIY" and tailor customers |
| **Conversion Drivers** | Quick purchase, fit assurance | Customization possibility, value |
