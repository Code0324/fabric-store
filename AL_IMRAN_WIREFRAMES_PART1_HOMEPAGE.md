## CONVERSION STRATEGY BY SECTION

### 1. Announcement Bar
**Purpose:** Immediate trust-building
**Conversion Impact:** +3-5% reduction in cart abandonment
**Key Messages:**
- Free shipping removes shipping concern
- COD addresses payment anxiety
- Sale creates urgency

**Mobile Note:** Horizontal scroll on mobile shows 2 messages at a time, refreshes every 5 sec

---

### 2. Sticky Header
**Purpose:** Navigation accessibility during scroll
**Conversion Impact:** +8-12% navigation efficiency
**Desktop Elements:**
- Logo (clickable to homepage)
- Search bar with autocomplete
- Cart count badge (motivates browsing)
- Wishlist icon (captures indecisive users)
- Account dropdown

**Mobile Elements:**
- Hamburger menu (tap to reveal full navigation)
- Search icon (tap to expand search)
- Compact cart badge
- No account dropdown (use account link in menu)
